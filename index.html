Reunion Road Trip: Northern Spain

Peter, Vivek, Sean, Kevin, Eric, Ruben, and Joaquin reunite after 3 years in Madrid!

Trip Overview

This is a special reunion road trip! Arriving in Madrid on Thursday, April 3rd, you’ll spend one night in the capital, then hit the road north to explore the green and historic regions of Northern Spain until Friday, April 11th. Most of you will return to Madrid and fly out on Sunday, April 13th. Enjoy a mix of cultural cities, rugged coastlines, mountain hikes, and delectable local cuisine along the way.

Weather in Northern Spain (April)

  • Mild & Variable: Daytime temperatures typically range from 15°C to 20°C (59–68°F), with cooler evenings and occasional rain showers.
  • Lush Scenery: Springtime brings a burst of greenery and blooming wildflowers, ideal for outdoor adventures.
  • Pack Layers: Bring a light raincoat and layers to adjust to the changing conditions.

Reunion Itinerary

The following detailed itinerary has been planned for your road trip north of Madrid:

  1. Thursday, April 3 – Madrid Reunion
    • Arrive in Madrid and check into your hotel.
    • Meet up for a reunion dinner in the city center (consider traditional Spanish cuisine in the Plaza Mayor area).
  2. Friday, April 4 – Madrid to Segovia & Burgos
    • Morning: Depart from Madrid and drive to Segovia (≈1 hour). Visit the Roman aqueduct and Alcázar.
    • Afternoon: Continue to Burgos (≈2 hours) to explore the Burgos Cathedral and enjoy local tapas.
    • Overnight in Burgos.
  3. Saturday, April 5 – Burgos to Bilbao
    • Morning: Brief exploration of Burgos (if time permits).
    • Afternoon: Drive to Bilbao (≈2 hours). Visit the Guggenheim Museum and sample Basque cuisine.
    • Overnight in Bilbao.
  4. Sunday, April 6 – Bilbao to San Sebastián
    • Morning: Explore more of Bilbao’s cultural spots.
    • Afternoon: Drive along the scenic Basque coast to San Sebastián (≈1 hour). Enjoy pintxos and stroll along La Concha beach.
    • Overnight in San Sebastián.
  5. Monday, April 7 – San Sebastián
    • Spend the day exploring San Sebastián’s old town and beaches.
    • Optional coastal hike or relaxing in a local café.
    • Overnight in San Sebastián.
  6. Tuesday, April 8 – San Sebastián to Oviedo (Asturias)
    • Morning: Depart San Sebastián for Oviedo (≈3 hours drive).
    • Afternoon: Explore Oviedo’s historic center, including the Cathedral and charming plazas.
    • Evening: Savor Asturian cuisine and local cider.
    • Overnight in Oviedo.
  7. Wednesday, April 9 – Oviedo & Picos de Europa
    • Early Morning: Drive toward the Picos de Europa region (≈1.5–2 hours to Covadonga).
    • Hike a portion of the Ruta del Cares or visit Lagos de Covadonga.
    • Return to Oviedo or stay overnight in a nearby rural guesthouse.
  8. Thursday, April 10 – Explore Picos de Europa
    • Spend a full day exploring mountain trails and charming villages in the Picos de Europa.
    • Enjoy group meals featuring local specialties.
    • Overnight in the Picos area or return to Oviedo.
  9. Friday, April 11 – Return to Madrid
    • Morning: Depart from Northern Spain back to Madrid (≈4–5 hours drive).
    • Afternoon: Arrive in Madrid; enjoy a farewell group dinner.
    • Overnight in Madrid.
  10. Saturday, April 12 – Leisure in Madrid
    • Free day to explore Madrid—visit museums, parks, or shop in local markets.
    • Overnight in Madrid.
  11. Sunday, April 13 – Departure
    • Most of the group will fly out of Madrid.

Planning & Travel Tips

  • Book Early: Reserve hotels and rental cars ahead of time as group bookings can fill up quickly.
  • Rental Car: A car or campervan is ideal for exploring remote regions and making multiple stops in one day.
  • Navigation: Use apps like Google Maps and BusBud (for public transport) to plan day trips and find the best routes.
  • Stay Flexible: While the itinerary is set, allow extra time for unexpected discoveries along the way!
